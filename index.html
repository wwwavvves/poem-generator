Threads of Memory

In the kitchen, a knife gleams,
a whisper of my father’s lessons.
Outside, the city pulses,
an orange warms in my hand.

A breeze murmurs dreams,
clouds form within.
In the garden, a single violet
trembles, a promise of spring.

Each moment, a thread,
weaving the fabric of who I am.
